Data Science in Practice

03/2022 - 06/2022

XAI Stories 3.0
I participated in the "Data Science in Practice" project during my BA in Econometrics and Data Analytics at the University of Lodz, the project was carried out in cooperation with McKinsey and the University of Warsaw. Its aim was to apply machine learning techniques to business practice. As a participant of the project, I worked with the group on chapter four, focused on the classification of customers due to their predisposition to make a loan application. The study aimed to identify the key factors influencing the decision to submit a loan application.

During the project, the classification models were compared and the best one was selected. A summary of key features was then prepared using black box model explanation techniques. SHAP, LIME and PDP methods were used to select the most relevant variables. These techniques enabled a better understanding of how individual variables affect the classification results. This allowed to draw conclusions about customers' decision-making processes in the finance sector.
