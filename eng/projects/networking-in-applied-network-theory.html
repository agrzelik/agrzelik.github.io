Networking in Applied Network Theory

10/2022 - 02/2023

NANT - website
As part of my participation in the event "Networking in Applied Network Theory", I worked on a project on financial contagions. The main aim of the project was to compare the classical approach to modelling the global stock market with an approach based on network analysis. Financial contagions refer to the process by which a financial crisis or failure of one financial institution can lead to a cascading effect, threatening the stability of the entire financial system. For several months, I participated in online mini-courses as preparation for the research project. Together with the team, we had the opportunity to explore the complexity of the global stock market network. The project ended with a workshop on network modelling in BÄ™dlewo in February 2023, where along with the team I presented the talk 'Financial contagions: classical versus network-based approach to predicting stock market crashes' and I also had the opportunity to discuss with experts in the field of network analysis. I am very happy to have had the opportunity to participate in this event, which not only contributed to my career development, but also opened up new perspectives in my research path.
